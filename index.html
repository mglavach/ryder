How It Works

Your Path to Gold

Simple rules, maximum fun. Here's how the day unfolds for every Ryder team.

1

Form Your Team

Rally your department crew. Every teammate counts toward your score — no one sits the bench.

2

Hit the Stations

Rotate through Ice-Breakers, Team Challenges, Classic Sports, and Brain Busters. Earn points at each stop.

3

Climb the Leaderboard

Points accumulate in real time. Watch your team rise — or fall — and strategize your way to the top.

4

Claim the Trophy

The team with the most points at the final whistle wins the Office Olympics crown and eternal bragging rights.
